Details are still emerging, but sources familiar with the probe have revealed troubling allegations:

This isn't the first intelligence leak scandal of the 2020s, but it may be the most consequential. Previous leaks have exposed operational plans, endangered agents in the field, and compromised diplomatic negotiations.

The investigation is ongoing, and no charges have been filed yet. But the damage to institutional credibility may already be done.
