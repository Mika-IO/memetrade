## The Madness Has Begun

The 2026 NCAA Tournament kicked off with a bang on Thursday, March 19, and brackets across America are already in flames. **Ohio State fell to TCU** in a stunning first-round upset, while **Nebraska won its first-ever men's NCAA Tournament game** in program history against Troy.

## The Bracket-Busting Storylines

This year's tournament is loaded with drama from the start:

* **Duke is the #1 overall seed**, led by Cameron Boozer, widely considered the best player in college basketball.
* **Florida is defending its title** and trying to become just the second school to win back-to-back championships multiple times.
* **South Florida**, an 11-seed on an 11-game winning streak, is this year's trendy Cinderella pick against Louisville.
* **VCU** has won 16 of its last 17 games and could upset North Carolina, which is without star freshman Caleb Wilson.

## The Upset Watch

Experts are eyeing several potential bracket busters. The 5-12 matchup is always dangerous — at least one 12-seed has won in five of the last six tournaments. This year, **Akron** in the Midwest Region is the popular dark horse pick.

Fill out your brackets wisely — and expect the unexpected.
